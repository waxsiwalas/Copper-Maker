CHARLES WILLIE-T̓łAKWAGILA

 

G̱ilakas'la, welcome in all your greatness.Copper Maker Designs is here to help you with all your digital creation needs: website designing/building, graphics, audio, video, multimedia content and language course interactive material.

 

I have many years of self taught audio restoration, mastering and live recordings. My passion for this came about me wanting to learn cereminal songs and chants of my lineage. I started out with audio, moved into photo restoration/editing. A few years go by and I started doing video recording and got into some minor animation. All of these different media, I started looking into multimedia delivery building. Website building is one way to deliver multimedia content, as well as software and app programing. I use mainly Adobe products to produce much of the material.

'Uik̓ala'
